# Project Overview

FinPlanIQ is a realistic FP&A analytics project built around a simulated mid-sized fintech/SaaS company. The dashboard helps leadership understand monthly performance against budget, identify revenue and expense variance drivers, evaluate department-level overspend, and test scenario impacts on EBITDA and margin.

## Target Roles

- Financial Analyst
- FP&A Analyst
- Finance Data Analyst
- BI Analyst
- Business Analyst in finance
- Risk/Reporting Analyst

## Business Questions

- Did revenue come in above or below budget?
- Which regions and business units are driving revenue variance?
- Which departments or cost centers are overspending?
- How are gross margin and EBITDA margin trending?
- What happens to profit if revenue drops or payroll costs increase?
- What should leadership know this month?
